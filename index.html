Obtained sources from Edmunds.com and YourMechanic.com by looking at FAQ’s, and articles that stated the average mileage expectancy for a particular car part. It is visualized it on a 1991 BMW M3. Of course, the parts can still be found in similar areas on other make and models but not all. We explain this more in “Let’s Expand”

Love for cars was the spark to the idea behind this data visualization.

On a larger scale, this visualization can be customized by user data (year, make, model) Reason by example would be that A FWD sedan will have different location for their car parts compared to a Mid Engine Bus. Obtaining user input on their mileage can also be a new field to explore as that opens the window of estimating what needs replacement.
